H3LLOWORLD is a project that brings together developers and designers.
You can create clothes with fabrics made of electronic paper and code them after connecting them to a PC.

WHY DAISY?

Why is the symbol of H3LLOWORLD the daisy?
The flower language of the daisy is beauty and innocence.
Decorate it as you wish and make it beautiful.

HOW TO MAKE?

H3LLOWORLD's electronic t-shirts are made through a three-step process.

Step 1: E-Paper Fabric Printing

After the designer finalizes the pattern on a PC, the pixel-level display is printed onto flexible fabric using e-paper ink.

Step 2: Circuit Integration

The core electronic circuit is mounted onto the printed e-paper fabric, and conductive ink traces are connected to the circuit via micro-soldering or adhesive bonding techniques.

Step 3: Final Inspection & Packaging

A QC specialist checks the finished electronic T-shirt against a checklist—verifying screen display, circuit connections, and durability.
